We are currently preparing for our next event, to be held in October 2020. In preparing for this events we have issued an artist call-out to any and all artists who are interested. Applications are now open.

past events:


Our first event, Rents Due, was held in London in Nov. 2017. We had the honor of hosting some amazingly talented young artists from all around the world. Many of whom were showcasing their work for the first time. Continuing on this success, we hosted our second event in Edinburgh last fall. Again, we were stunned by the quality of our artists and loved providing a platform for passionate young people to shine.
