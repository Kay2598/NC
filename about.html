About us:

Founded by Eliza Rennie and George Marriott in 2016, northstreet.collective was designed to create a space in which student artists could showcase their work in a professional, friendly capacity. The collective takes its name from North Street, the name of the street on which the arts building sits in St. Andrews. Both Eliza and George study Art History at the University of St. Andrews in this building on North Street and thus were inspired to use this local, where thier frinedship and shared love of art was founded, as the name for their collaborative collective.
